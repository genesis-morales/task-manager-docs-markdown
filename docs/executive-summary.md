# TaskManager App - Product Plan

## Executive Summary

The TaskManager App is a comprehensive web-based task and project management solution designed to help individuals and teams organize, track, and complete their work efficiently. This product plan outlines the strategic approach to deliver a robust, scalable, and user-friendly application through incremental releases.

### Vision

To provide users with an intuitive, powerful task management platform that combines project organization, deadline tracking, and seamless third-party integrations to enhance productivity.

### Business Objectives

1. Deliver a functional MVP within 8-10 weeks
2. Achieve user adoption through intuitive UX and responsive design
3. Enable seamless integration with popular productivity tools
4. Build a scalable foundation for future feature expansion
5. Maintain high security standards for user data protection

### Target Audience

- **Primary**: Individual professionals and freelancers managing multiple projects
- **Secondary**: Small teams (3-10 members) needing lightweight project coordination
- **Future**: Medium-sized teams requiring advanced collaboration features
